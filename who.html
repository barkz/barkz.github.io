The Story

WuBu, LLC is a small independent software studio focused on thoughtful, useful, and occasionally playful software for Apple platforms. We build the kinds of apps we want to use ourselves — tools that get out of your way and games that respect your time.

What We Make

Our work falls into two buckets:

  • macOS utilities that sharpen everyday workflows for power users and makers.
  • iPhone games built with care, playable in short bursts, without ads or dark patterns.

How We Work

We ship slowly and deliberately. We prefer polished releases over feature creep, and clear pricing over data collection. If something feels off, we'd rather take it down than leave it broken.
