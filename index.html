CLOCKTASTIC

Releasing on June 20

Clocktastic brings the charm of a retro bedside clock straight to your Mac menu bar. Choose from a library of pixel-perfect clock faces, each one hand-crafted with nostalgic flair. Multiple time zones, alarms, and focus timers are always a single click away. Because checking the time should feel good.

LIGHTSOUT

Releasing July 8

LightsOut is a minimalist puzzle game built around a single rule: tap a tile and its neighbors flip. Start with a lit grid and work your way to darkness, one clever move at a time. Hundreds of handcrafted puzzles, offline play, and zero ads getting in your way. Quick to learn, tough to master.

SHARED STICKIES

Releasing July 24

Shared Stickies is a collaborative take on the humble desktop sticky note. Drop a note on your Mac and it shows up instantly on everyone you've shared it with. Rich text, color tags, and simple permissions keep teams aligned without yet another chat tool. The easiest way to leave a note for someone across the room or across the world.
