Overview

WuBu, LLC ("we", "us", or "our") builds macOS utilities and iPhone games. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about what information is collected and how it is used. This policy explains our practices for our website and applications.

Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little information as possible. Depending on the product or interaction, we may collect:

  • Contact information you choose to share with us (such as an email address when you reach out for support).
  • Device and diagnostic data that our apps may report to identify crashes or performance issues. This data is anonymized where possible.
  • Gameplay or usage data stored locally on your device so that our apps can function correctly.

Our website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that identify individual visitors.

How We Use Information

Any information we receive is used solely to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our apps and services.
  • Respond to support requests or feedback you send us.
  • Diagnose and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your information with third parties for advertising purposes. Information may only be disclosed when required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Storage

Game progress, preferences, and app settings are generally stored on your device. Where applicable, Apple's iCloud services may be used to sync data across your own devices, governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Your Rights

You may request that we provide, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our products after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please reach out to rob.barker@outlook.com.
