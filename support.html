Need Help?

We're a small studio and we read every message. Whether something isn't working, you're stuck on a level, or you have an idea you'd like us to consider — we'd love to hear from you.

Before You Reach Out

To help us help you faster, please include:

  • The name and version of the app you're using.
  • Your device and OS version (e.g. MacBook Air M2 on macOS 15.2, or iPhone 15 on iOS 18).
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected.
  • A screenshot or screen recording if it's a visual issue.

Response Times

We aim to reply within two business days. Complex bugs may take longer to diagnose, but we'll always acknowledge your message and keep you posted as we investigate.

Feature Requests

We love hearing what you'd like to see next. Every request is read, though we can't promise every idea will make it into a build. If your idea lines up with where an app is headed, we'll tell you.

Refunds & Purchases

Purchases made through the App Store are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with the Apple ID used to buy the app.
